Our Story

BUILT FROM
THE GROUND
UP

"We don't train fighters.
We build warriors."

Ground Born Fight Club was born from a singular vision — to create an elite combat training environment in Mangalore that rivals the world's best. We are not a gym. We are a movement. A brotherhood forged in sweat, discipline, and relentless purpose.

From beginners stepping onto the mat for the first time to seasoned competitors preparing for national championships — every athlete is treated with the same standard of excellence.
